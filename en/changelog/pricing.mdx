title: "Pricing Updates"
---

<Note>
Pricing changes apply only to calls made **after** the change takes effect; historical calls are unaffected. Each model's live unit price is shown in the [Models overview](/en/docs/models) and the console "Model Market"; the cost of each call is authoritative as returned in `cost` / `usage.cost`.
</Note>

<Update label="2026-06-24" description="Pricing change">
**Seedance 2.0 pricing synced to official**

- `doubao-seedance-2.0` unit prices are now synced to official rates, and the new **4K** tier is live for billing. Current prices are shown in the [Models overview](/en/docs/models) and the console "Model Market".
</Update>

<Update label="2026-06-17" description="Pricing change">
**Pricing synced for some video models**

- Unit prices for some video models were synced to official rates. Current prices are shown in the [Models overview](/en/docs/models) and the console "Model Market".
</Update>
