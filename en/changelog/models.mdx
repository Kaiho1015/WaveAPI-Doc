<Update label="2026-06-17" description="Model launch">
**Kling 3.0 Turbo · Video generation**

- **Model ID**: `kling-3.0-turbo`
- **Modes**: text-to-video, image-to-video (pass `image_urls` as the first frame)
- **Resolution**: 720P / 1080P　·　**Duration**: 5 / 10 s　·　**Aspect ratios**: 16:9 / 9:16 / 1:1
- **Docs**: [Kling video API](/en/api-reference/video/kling)

**Grok Imagine 1.5 · Image / edit / video**

- **Image**　`grok-imagine-1.5` — text-to-image, aspect ratios 1:1 / 16:9 / 9:16 / 3:2 / 2:3, batch `n`
- **Edit**　`grok-imagine-1.5-edit` — generate / edit, pass `image_urls` as references
- **Video**　`grok-imagine-1.5-video` — text/image-to-video, resolution 480p / 720p, duration 6–30 s, ratios 16:9 / 9:16 / 1:1
- **Docs**: [Grok Imagine image API](/en/api-reference/image/grok-imagine)　·　[Grok Imagine video API](/en/api-reference/video/grok-imagine-video)
</Update>
